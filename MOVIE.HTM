About the Movie

This movie is based on the book made by Beatrix Potter.
In this movie Peter and his friends find they have a new neighbor moving in, Thomas McGregor, grand nephew of Mr McGregor, Peter's enemy.

Peter and his friends must stop Thomas McGregor before they're locked out from the garden forever!
